How to play

Positive pitch is an app to help you train your musical ear. When you press play, you will be played a chord with the selected number of notes. You can select the notes that you think the chord contains and submit, or have the chord repeated. After you submit, the correct chord is displayed in orange.

How it works

The app uses the HTML5 <audio> tag to load audio. Javascript & jQuery do the rest. Have a look at the GitHub repository if you like.
